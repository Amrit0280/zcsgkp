A Joyful Space Where Tiny Dreams Take Flight 🌟

Kilkariyan Playway School is a joyful learning space under the banner of Zenith Convent School, Nandanagar, Gorakhpur — where young minds explore, create, and grow in an atmosphere filled with curiosity, laughter, and discovery.

We believe that early childhood education should be a celebration of imagination. Our play-based curriculum is thoughtfully designed to nurture every child's natural love of learning — through stories, songs, games, and hands-on activities that build confidence, creativity, and essential life skills.
